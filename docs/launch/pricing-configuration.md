# UTM Builder - Pricing Configuration & Strategy

## Gumroad Pricing Setup

### Primary Configuration
- **Base Price:** $19 USD
- **Pricing Model:** Pay What You Want (PWYW)
- **Minimum Price:** $19
- **Suggested Amounts:** $19, $29, $49

### Pricing Rationale

**$19 Base Price Justification:**
- **Time savings value:** 20+ minutes per campaign × $50/hour rate = $16.67 value per use
- **Error prevention:** Prevents GA4 miscategorization issues (hours of debugging time)
- **Professional template:** Based on agency best practices and 6 months of testing
- **Competitive positioning:** Below $29-49 range of similar marketing templates
- **Psychological pricing:** $19 feels accessible while maintaining value perception

**Pay What You Want Strategy:**
- **Removes price objection:** Eliminates "too expensive" barrier
- **Value-based pricing:** Customers pay based on perceived value
- **Higher lifetime value:** Happy customers often pay more than asking price
- **Positive reviews:** PWYW customers more likely to leave good reviews
- **Market research:** Learn customer willingness-to-pay without A/B testing

## Competitive Analysis

### Direct Competitors
**Google's UTM Builder (Free):**
- Pro: Free, official Google tool
- Con: One-off URLs only, no naming convention, no team standards

**UTM.io ($15/month):**
- Pro: Web-based, team collaboration
- Con: Monthly subscription, $180/year ongoing cost

**Campaign URL Builder Tools ($5-25 one-time):**
- Pro: Low cost, simple functionality
- Con: Basic features, no GA4 alignment, limited customization

### Competitive Positioning
- **Vs. Free tools:** More comprehensive, systematic approach, GA4 alignment
- **Vs. Subscription tools:** One-time purchase, no ongoing costs, offline capability
- **Vs. Basic builders:** Professional naming framework, channel examples, bulk building

## Pricing Experiments & Optimization

### A/B Test Ideas (Future)
1. **$19 vs $29 fixed pricing** (remove PWYW)
2. **Bundle pricing** (UTM Builder + other templates)
3. **Tiered pricing** (Basic $19, Pro $39 with extras)

### Success Metrics to Track
- **Average transaction value** (target: $22-25)
- **Conversion rate** (product page to purchase)
- **Customer reviews/satisfaction** (4.5+ stars)
- **Refund rate** (target: <5%)
- **PWYW vs. minimum price distribution**

### Price Increase Triggers
- **High demand:** 100+ sales in first month
- **Strong reviews:** 4.7+ star average with 20+ reviews
- **Feature additions:** New tabs or significant template updates
- **Market validation:** Similar products launching at higher prices
