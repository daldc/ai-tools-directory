**📊 UTM Builder**
Fill in your URL, source, medium, and campaign — the tagged URL generates automatically. Dropdown menus for common sources and mediums eliminate typos. Space for 50+ URLs per session.

**📋 Campaign Naming Convention Framework**
A complete system for naming campaigns across every channel. Includes the naming formula, 6 golden rules, and **14 channel-specific examples** covering:
- Google Ads & Microsoft Ads
- Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok
- Email marketing & newsletters
- Affiliate & influencer campaigns
- Organic social & content marketing

**🎯 GA4 Channel Group Alignment**
Stop traffic from showing up as "Other." This reference maps every `utm_medium` value to its corresponding GA4 default channel group. Know exactly what to use — every time.

**⚡ Bulk URL Builder**
Launching across 10 channels? Enter your base URL once and generate all tagged URLs simultaneously. Pre-configured with common channel setups.

**🚀 Quick Start Guide**
Working in under 5 minutes with step-by-step instructions, parameter explanations, and pro tips for your whole team.

Includes guidance on integrating with Bitly, Rebrandly, Short.io, and YOURLS — get clean, branded links without breaking UTM tracking.
