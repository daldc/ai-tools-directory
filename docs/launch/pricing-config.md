# UTM Builder — Pricing & Gumroad Configuration

## Pricing

| Setting | Value |
|---------|-------|
| **Suggested price** | $19 |
| **Minimum price** | $0+ (Pay What You Want) |
| **Model** | PWYW — buyer chooses, $19 pre-filled |

### Rationale

- $19 is the anchor price — high enough to signal professional quality, low enough to be an impulse buy for marketers.
- PWYW lowers friction for first-time buyers and students while letting power users and agencies pay more if they see the value.
- Gumroad pre-fills the suggested price, so most buyers will pay $19 or close to it.

## Gumroad Product Settings

| Setting | Value |
