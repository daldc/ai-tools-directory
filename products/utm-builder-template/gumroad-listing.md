## Full Product Description

### The Problem

You've been there. Your Google Analytics shows half your paid traffic as "Other" because someone typed `Social` instead of `paid-social`. Your campaign reports are a mess because every team member names campaigns differently. And you're wasting 20 minutes per campaign manually building UTM URLs.

**It doesn't have to be this way.**

### What You Get

A professional, ready-to-use Google Sheets template with **5 purpose-built tabs**:

**📊 UTM Builder**
Fill in your URL, source, medium, and campaign — the tagged URL generates automatically. Dropdown menus for common sources and mediums keep your team consistent. Room for 50+ URLs per session.

**📋 Campaign Naming Convention Framework**
A complete system for naming campaigns across every channel. Includes the naming formula, 6 golden rules, and **14 channel-specific examples** covering Google Ads, Facebook, LinkedIn, TikTok, email, affiliate, and more.

**🎯 GA4 Channel Group Alignment Guide**
Stop your traffic from showing up as "Other." This reference tab maps every `utm_medium` value to its corresponding GA4 default channel group. Know exactly what to use — every time.

**⚡ Bulk URL Builder**
Launching across multiple channels? Enter your base URL once and generate tagged URLs for 10+ channels simultaneously. Pre-filled with common channel configurations.

**🚀 Quick Start Guide**
Get up and running in under 5 minutes with step-by-step instructions, parameter explanations, and pro tips.

**🔗 Link Shortener Integration Notes**
Includes guidance on pairing your tagged URLs with Bitly, Rebrandly, Short.io, and YOURLS — so you get clean, branded links without breaking UTM tracking.
