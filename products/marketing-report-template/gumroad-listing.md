### What's Inside

📊 **Executive Summary Tab**
Start with what matters. Pre-built layout for top-line KPIs, period-over-period changes, and traffic-light status indicators so stakeholders get the picture in 10 seconds.

📈 **Channel Performance Breakdown**
Eight marketing channels pre-configured with the metrics that actually matter: spend, impressions, clicks, CTR, conversions, CPA, revenue, and ROAS. Works for any channel mix — just hide what you don't use.

🎯 **KPI Dashboard**
Targets vs. actuals with automatic variance calculations. Conditional formatting baked in so green = good, red = needs attention. No formulas to build — just plug in your numbers.

📅 **Monthly Trend Analysis**
12-month rolling view with month-over-month and year-over-year comparison columns. Designed for sparklines and charts. Spot seasonality and momentum at a glance.

💡 **Recommendations Framework**
This is where most reports fall flat. Our structured format guides you through: Observation → Data Evidence → Recommendation → Expected Impact → Priority Level. Turn "the numbers went up" into actionable strategy.

📖 **Setup & Instructions**
Metric definitions glossary, customization guide, and tips for making the template your own. No guesswork.
